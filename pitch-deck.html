02 / 07 · Who we are
— The authority

Seven decades
of Malaysian
motorsport.

MAM is the only Malaysian body recognised by the FIA and FIM Asia. We sanction every form of motorsport in Malaysia — from grassroots karting at Tangkak to the Asia Road Racing Championship at Sepang.

We license drivers, riders, officials and entrants. We issue event permits, write the rule book, and represent Malaysia at the international tables where motorsport is governed.

Founded in 1956 as the country's first sanctioning body, we have written every iteration of the National Sporting Code that has shaped Malaysian motorsport.

06 / 07 · What we do
— The work of the federation

Three pillars,
one mandate.

01 / Sanction

Permit every event.

Every sanctioned event in Malaysia operates under MAM permit — issued only when supplementary regulations, risk assessments, medical cover and insurance are in order.

  • Permit applications62 / yr
  • Disciplines covered12+
  • Lead time≥ 60 days
02 / License

License every competitor.

Driver, rider, official and entrant licences — graded from National Junior to FIA International A. Recognised across the FIA's 80+ member federations and the FIM Asia region.

  • Active licences2,400
  • Grades issuedD → A
  • Renewal cycleAnnual
03 / Govern

Write the rule book.

The National Sporting Code, technical regulations and penalty schedules — published, maintained and aligned with the FIA International Sporting Code and FIM standards.
