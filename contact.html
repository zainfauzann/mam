Common questions

Before you reach out

How do I apply for a competition licence? −
Download the relevant form from the Downloads page (Driver / Rider / Officials), complete the FIA Annex L medical, and submit to the MAM Secretariat. Processing takes 10–14 working days. New applicants must complete a one-day induction at HQ.
What is the lead time to apply for an event permit? +
Minimum 60 days before the event date. Permits include the application, supplementary regulations, risk assessment and proof of insurance. International events require 90 days.
Is my Malaysian licence valid in other countries? +
Yes — MAM-issued FIA International Grades are recognised across all 80+ FIA member federations worldwide. FIM Asia road racing licences are recognised across the FIM Asia region.
How do I become an MAM official or steward? +
Officials' licences are graded D → A. Apply for Grade D after attending an introductory clinic. Progression requires logged event hours and recommendation from a discipline commission.
Can my club affiliate with MAM? +
Yes. Clubs that organise sanctioned events or promote driver/rider development may apply for affiliate status. The application includes proof of registration with the Sports Commissioner, by-laws, and a list of office bearers.
