Step-by-step

How to apply

Download printable guide ↓
01 / Start

Open the MAM Licence Portal

Go to license-mam.com and select the application type that matches your case: New Application, Renew Licence, or Parent (for applicants below 18). Make sure your email address is valid — every confirmation in the process is delivered to that address.

Online only
02 / Form

Fill out the application form

Complete the four-step form: Application → Personal Details → Licence Category → Download Form. Confirm the captcha and submit. On completion, click "Download My Medical Form" to get the pre-filled medical PDF — the same form is also sent to your email.

~ 8 minutes
03 / Medical

Complete the medical check-up

Take the downloaded medical form to any clinic or hospital for examination — there are no panel clinics. The form must be chopped and signed. Two clinics are excluded: Poliklinik Permai USJ and Klinik Mesra Johor Bahru. Applicants aged 45+ must additionally complete a stress test at any nearby clinic or hospital.

Any clinic (with 2 exclusions)
04 / Test

Take the Competition Licence Test

Before uploading documents, complete a 20-question Competition Licence Test on the portal. Pass mark is 80% (16 out of 20). The test covers race flags, signals, scrutineering and the National Sporting Code. All questions must be answered.

20 questions · 80% pass
05 / Upload

Upload your documents

Upload the signed medical form, club affiliation receipt/proof, passport-size photo (white background), IC/Passport copy and JPJ driving licence. Any other supporting paperwork can be attached as "additional supporting documents." Use the delete (×) button to correct any mis-uploaded file.

5 required + extras
06 / Pay & collect

Approval, payment and collection

A MAM Administrator reviews your application and emails you with your login details and an invoice link. Complete payment online. Once paid, collect your licence card at a MAM office or have it delivered to your next event. The licence is ready in 2 – 3 working days.

2 – 3 working days

FAQ

Common questions

How do I apply for a MAM Competition Licence?

Applications are submitted online through the MAM Licence Portal at license-mam.com. After filling out the form, you'll download a pre-filled medical form, complete it at any clinic, then upload your documents and take the Competition Licence Test through the portal.

How long will it take to complete the application process?

2 – 3 working days from approved payment to a ready licence card. You'll receive an email with login details and the invoice link after a MAM Administrator approves your uploaded documents.

Does MAM have any panel clinics for the medical?

No. You can have your medical check-up done at any clinic or hospital of your choice. There are only two exclusions: Poliklinik Permai USJ and Klinik Mesra Johor Bahru. The medical form must be chopped and signed by the examining doctor.

I'm over 45 — can I still apply?

Yes. Applicants aged 45 and above must additionally complete a stress test at any nearby clinic or hospital. Bring the result together with your standard medical form when you upload to the portal.

What documents do I need to upload?

1. Completed Medical Examination Form (downloaded from the portal). 2. Club affiliation receipt or proof of MAM affiliate membership. 3. Passport-size personal photo (white background). 4. Picture / scan of IC or passport. 5. JPJ driving licence copy (if any). Other supporting paperwork can be attached as additional supporting documents.

Where can I collect my licence card?

At either MAM office: Tingkat 1, Paddock Office, Sepang International Circuit, Selangor or Tingkat 2, The Nizra Building, No. 8, Jalan Sri Penchala, Sungai Penchala, Kuala Lumpur. You can also request delivery to your next race event.

What is MAM's bank account for licence payment?

Maybank — Persatuan Sukan Bermotor Malaysia (MAM). Account number: 5623 5753 4201. Payment is usually completed via the invoice link emailed after admin approval; the bank account is provided for direct transfers if needed.
