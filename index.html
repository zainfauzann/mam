MAM at a glance

The national authority for
Malaysian motorsport.

Incorporated on 28 February 2007 and recognised by the Ministry of Youth and Sports, MAM is officially sanctioned by the FIA and FIM to govern every form of four-wheel and two-wheel motorsport in Malaysia — from grassroots karting to the Asia Road Racing Championship.

National Sporting Authority

MAM is the National Sporting Authority recognised by the FIA and FIM.
