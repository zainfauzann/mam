Why affiliate

One federation,
every benefit.

Affiliation with the Motorsports Association of Malaysia gives clubs and organisers a single home under Malaysia's only FIA- and FIM-recognised sporting authority. Every internationally counted result — every championship podium, every licence, every record — flows through MAM.

Affiliated clubs sanction events under MAM permits, access standardised safety and technical regulations, and qualify for federation insurance cover for marshals and competitors. Once affiliated, each event you host is inscribed onto the MAM calendar under the rate card below.

"Affiliation isn't paperwork. It's the difference between an informal track day and a sanctioned race that counts."

The rate card below covers inscription, permit, stewards and insurance for Temporary & Permanent Circuit events. Other disciplines (rally, karting, motocross, drag) have their own rate cards available from the Documents archive. Individual driver and rider competition licences are a separate process — see Competition Licence.

Affiliation

Affiliate Member application process

Review: 3 – 5 working days · Probation: 1 year
No Voting Rights
01

New Application

  • Affiliated membership is open to clubs, associations or any organisation that the Executive Committee deems fit and relevant.
  • Each application must be nominated by an Affiliate and seconded by another Affiliate.
Procedure for application

New applicants must submit a written application for membership to the Secretary General with the following documents:

  1. Completed Application Form
  2. Club Profile with list of members
  3. Certificate of Registration with PJS (for Clubs / Associations — Form 3)
  4. Certificate of Incorporation (for Companies — Form 9)
  5. Latest Form 24, 44 & 49 (for Companies & Circuit Owners)
  6. Latest Bank Statement
  7. 3 years Report of Past Events, Programs & Activities (if any)

Application review takes 3 – 5 working days. Results are notified via email. Certificate of Membership is only issued upon receipt of full payment of the Affiliation Fee.

No Voting Rights
02

Probation (1 Year)

  • New Clubs / Associations undergo a one (1) year probationary period, commencing from the date of acceptance of the terms & conditions and payment of the Affiliation Fee.
  • During probation, the club must organise and submit a written report on at least three (3) Motorsports Activities — racing and/or activities promoting motorsport through exhibitions, seminars, classes or training.
  • All Motorsports Activities must be in strict compliance with the Constitution and all relevant laws, rules, regulations and guidelines.
  • Clubs / Associations without a track record of organising races are strictly monitored by MAM.
End of probation

The Executive Committee evaluates the club's performance and has absolute discretion to either:

  1. Approve the membership to Full Affiliated status; OR
  2. Extend the probation period.
Voting Rights
03

Affiliate Member

  • Awarded full affiliate status — deemed a Voting Member.
  • Required to organise, conduct and maintain a minimum of three (3) Motorsports Activities per calendar year. Failure may trigger Executive Committee review of full affiliate status.
  • All Motorsports Activities must remain in strict compliance with the Constitution, relevant laws, rules, regulations and guidelines.
  • Annual membership renewal and fees due on or before 31 January each year. Failure to meet the deadline results in automatic suspension unless MAM determines otherwise.
  • Each member is encouraged to propose new members to MAM.
