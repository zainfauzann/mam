Why affiliate

One federation,
every benefit.

Affiliation with the Motorsports Association of Malaysia gives clubs, drivers, riders and officials a single home under Malaysia's only FIA- and FIM-recognised sporting authority. Every internationally counted result — every championship podium, every licence, every record — flows through MAM.

Affiliated clubs sanction events under MAM permits, access standardised safety and technical regulations, and qualify for federation insurance cover for marshals and competitors. Individual driver and rider inscriptions unlock the national licence ladder — from grade C through to international FIA / FIM grades.

"Affiliation isn't paperwork. It's the difference between an informal track day and a sanctioned race that counts."

Inscription runs on an annual cycle aligned to the calendar year. Fees are set by the Executive Committee and ratified at the AGM. The schedule below covers 2026 — printable copies are available from the Documents archive.

Affiliation

MAM affiliation process

≈ 8 weeks end-to-end
01 / Eligibility

Confirm eligibility

Motor clubs must be registered with the Registrar of Societies (ROS), demonstrate at least six months of operation, and have a constitution covering motorsport. Drivers and riders must be 14 and above (kart) or 16 and above (full licence) with valid road licences where applicable.

Self-check
02 / Submission

Submit the affiliation pack

Complete the club affiliation form (Form CAF-26) and submit the supporting pack: ROS certificate, constitution and bylaws, current office bearers roster, AGM minutes from the past two years, and audited financials. Individual licences submit the driver / rider application with medical and ID.

Forms in Documents
03 / Review

Secretariat review

The MAM Secretariat reviews documentation for completeness. Clarifications are typically requested within 14 working days. Officials licences route additionally through the Officials Commission for grading.

≈ 2 weeks
04 / Approval

Executive Committee approval

The Executive Committee ratifies affiliation at the next sitting (usually monthly). Successful applicants receive a confirmation letter, affiliation number, and inscription invoice.

Monthly EXCO
05 / Onboarding

Payment & onboarding

On payment of inscription, MAM issues the affiliate ID and adds the club / individual to the federation roster. Affiliates receive the Sporting Code, technical regulations, and Officials' Clinic schedule. Clubs gain access to the event-permit portal.

MAM ID issued
06 / Renewal

Annual renewal

Inscription runs on the calendar year. Renewal opens 1 December and closes 31 January with a -20% early-bird discount; late renewals after 28 February incur a reinstatement fee. AGM voting eligibility requires inscription confirmed by 31 March.

A — Eligibility

Who can apply

  • Motor clubs — ROS-registered, 6+ months active, motorsport in constitution
  • Drivers / Riders — 14+ (karting), 16+ (full national licence)
  • Officials — completed Officials Clinic, current medical
  • Entrants & teams — registered business entity, minimum one licensed driver / rider
B — Documents

Required documents

  1. Form CAF-26 (clubs) or DCL-26 / RCL-26 (individuals)
  2. ROS certificate & constitution (clubs)
  3. Office bearer roster & recent AGM minutes (clubs)
  4. FIA Annex L medical (4-wheel) or FIM medical (2-wheel)
  5. Two passport photographs & ID copy
  6. Inscription fee receipt or proof of payment
